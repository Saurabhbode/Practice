Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favouraite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

    • Method

  • Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  • Gently mix the eggs and vream in a bowl
  • once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  • Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  • When the egg are still moist remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
